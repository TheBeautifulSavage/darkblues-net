Dark Blues Artists

The key voices of dark blues — from the Delta to the present day

The Originators

Robert Johnson — The patron saint of dark blues. 29 recordings. The devil at the crossroads. Every dark blues artist lives in his shadow.
Howlin' Wolf — Chester Burnett's voice was a force of nature. "Smokestack Lightning," "Spoonful," "Back Door Man" — dark blues at its most elemental.
Son House — Raw, anguished, confrontational. His "Death Letter Blues" remains one of the most harrowing songs in American music.

The Modern Voice

Dark Country Boy — With 70 albums and 1,481 songs, Dark Country Boy bridges dark blues and dark country in a catalog unlike anything else in independent music. Raw vocals, sparse arrangements, unflinching lyrics.
